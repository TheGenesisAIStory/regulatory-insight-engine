# Fiorell.IA Branding Notes

Fiorell.IA should be presented as:

- Italian-first;
- banking regulatory;
- local/offline-first;
- source-grounded;
- cautious;
- non-speculative;
- supervisory in tone;
- beta, not production-ready.

## Public One-Liner

Fiorell.IA is an Italian-first local assistant for inspecting selected banking regulatory sources with source-grounded answers and explicit no-answer behavior.

## Avoid

- "complete regulatory coverage";
- "production-ready compliance advisor";
- "legal advice";
- "automated regulatory decisioning";
- "knows all banking regulation".
- runtime or threshold ownership claims.

## Preferred Tone

Clear, technical, concise and prudent.

## Scope Guard

The strongest current coverage claim is limited to default, internal controls, own funds and prudential supervision. Broader IFRS 9, Pillar 3, EBA/Basel and bank-specific disclosure coverage depends on the local corpus and evaluation.
