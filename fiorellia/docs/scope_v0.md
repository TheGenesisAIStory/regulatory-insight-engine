# Fiorell.IA Scope v0

## Release Positioning

Fiorell.IA v0 should be positioned as a beta assistant for local inspection of selected banking regulatory sources, not as a complete regulatory knowledge system.

Fiorell.IA v0 is a product/specification layer. Runtime ownership, serving behavior and retrieval configuration remain in the shared backend.

## Stronger Initial Coverage

The current corpus is strongest around:

- default and credit-risk regulatory concepts;
- internal controls and prudential supervision;
- own funds and capital terminology;
- Banca d'Italia supervision materials already present locally.

## Developing Coverage

The following areas require more corpus work before strong claims:

- full IFRS 9 coverage;
- broad Pillar 3 disclosure coverage;
- consolidated EBA/Basel frameworks across all relevant updates;
- bank-specific disclosure packs for Intesa Sanpaolo and other Italian banks;
- current facts that change by year, quarter or filing date.

## Out Of Scope For v0

Fiorell.IA v0 should not answer:

- investment recommendations;
- market forecasts;
- tax optimization;
- HR/employment questions;
- generic corporate finance;
- broad legal advice not tied to indexed sources;
- questions requiring documents not present in the local corpus.

## Acceptance Statement

A v0 answer is acceptable only when it is:

- in scope;
- supported by retrieved source context;
- written primarily in Italian;
- concise and cautious;
- traceable to local citations;
- willing to abstain when evidence is insufficient.
